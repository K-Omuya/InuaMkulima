Frequently Asked Questions

What is AgriConnect? +
AgriConnect is a platform that connects farmers with technology, helping them increase productivity and market access.
How can I register as a farmer? +
You can register by visiting our sign-up page and filling in your farming details.
Does AgriConnect provide financial support? +
Yes, we offer financing options and partnerships with agricultural lenders to support farmers.
Is there a mobile app for AgriConnect? +
Yes, we have a mobile app available on both Android and iOS for easy access to farming tools and information.
How can I contact AgriConnect support? +
You can reach out to our support team via email at support@agrictconnect.com or call us at +254 700 123 456.
What crops does AgriConnect support? +
We support a wide variety of crops, including maize, wheat, coffee, tea, and horticultural produce.
Can I access weather forecasts on AgriConnect? +
Yes, we provide real-time weather forecasts to help farmers plan their activities better.
